What is Algorithm?

Meaning -   a process or set of rules to be followed in calculations or other problem-solving
            operations, especially by a computer.

Wikipedia - In mathematics and computer science, an algorithm is an unambiguous specification
            of how to solve a class of problems. Algorithms can perform calculation,
            data processing, automated reasoning, and other tasks.
